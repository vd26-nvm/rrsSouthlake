Inspiring Impact Collective

Inspiring Impact Collective is a feature on Restoring Rainbows Southlake designed to highlight and celebrate the efforts and ideas of individuals within our community who are making a difference. This feature aims to inspire others to take action and contribute to these important causes.

Guidelines

Focus

Writing pieces and art forms should align with the values and mission of Restoring Rainbows Southlake. We will focus on three main categories related to our core values:


1) Environmental Advocacy

2) Education Crisis

3) Community Service
