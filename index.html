Who we are

We are the Southlake Branch of Restoring Rainbows, a youth-led non-profit organization! Restoring Rainbows is a a 501(c)(3) international nonprofit that collects, recycles and donates school supplies. We're so glad to start our own branch of Restoring Rainbows branch, and we would like to formally introduce our branch on this platform.


Our Story



Restoring Rainbows Southlake is dedicated to providing school supplies to those in need. Since our inception in 2024, we have been committed to making a positive impact in the community. Contact us today to learn more about our initiatives.
